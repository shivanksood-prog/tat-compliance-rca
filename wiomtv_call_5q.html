5-Question Caller Sheet

A tight version for fast calls. Same Jobs-to-be-Done lens — ask about real, recent moments; don't pitch; dig on emotion; write channel names down exactly.

Objective: understand what's working for the people already using Wiom TV.

Intro — open the call

"Namaste, main Wiom TV se [name] bol raha/rahi hoon. Aapka 5 minute le sakta/sakti hoon? Hum bas samajhna chahte hain ki ghar mein Wiom TV kaisa chal raha hai — koi sahi ya galat jawab nahi hai, aap jo bhi sach mein karte hain wahi sunna hai." (Warm, no-pitch. Make it clear you're here to learn, not to sell or fix anything.)
  • Ask about the past, not the hypothetical — "last time you watched", never "would you watch if…".
  • Don't pitch or lead. Be a curious listener. After they answer, stay silent — the second sentence is the honest one.
  • Dig on emotion & effort — when they show feeling or mention something they did, ask "why" 2–3 times.
  • Write channel/show names verbatim — exact titles are the strongest signal of the job.
1
Context · the old way
Who all watch TV at home — and before Wiom TV, how did everyone watch TV or shows?
घर में TV कौन-कौन देखता है — और Wiom TV से पहले सब TV या शो कैसे देखते थे? (cable, mobile, YouTube?)
Listen for: who's in the household, and the thing Wiom TV replaced (that's half the job).
2
The habit · last time watched
Tell me about the last time you watched — when was it, what time of day, who was in the room, and what did you put on first?
पिछली बार जब देखा — कब था, किस समय, कमरे में कौन था, और सबसे पहले क्या लगाया?
Then: on a normal day, when does Wiom TV usually get switched on, and what for? (who + when + what-first reveals the real job)
3
What's loved most
What does your family enjoy most about it? Which channels or shows? (write the names down exactly)
आपको या परिवार को इसमें सबसे ज़्यादा क्या अच्छा लगता है? कौन से channel या show? (नाम ज़रूर लिखें)
Dig: "and why is that good for you?" 2–3 times — until you hit a human outcome (peace, bachche busy, paisa bacha, cable jaisa lagta hai).
4
The unique value
What does Wiom TV let you do that you couldn't do before — and why does that matter to you?
Wiom TV से आप ऐसा क्या कर पाते हैं जो पहले नहीं कर पाते थे — और वो आपके लिए क्यों ज़रूरी है?
Listen for: the need being fulfilled — the gap in the old way that Wiom TV now fills.
5
The "miss it" test · proves importance
If Wiom TV stopped working tomorrow, what would you do instead — and what would you miss the most?
अगर कल Wiom TV बंद हो जाए, तो उसकी जगह आप क्या करेंगे — और सबसे ज़्यादा किसकी कमी खलेगी?
Listen for: strong emotion = a real job; a shrug = a weak one. What they'd switch back to reveals the true competitor.

Closing — feedback & thanks

"Aakhri sawaal — agar ek cheez Wiom TV mein jod sakte, toh kya jodte? Aur kuch aur jo maine nahi poocha par aap batana chahein?" "If you could add one thing to Wiom TV, what would it be? And anything I didn't ask that you'd like to tell me?" — note it, don't promise anything. Then: "Bahut bahut dhanyavaad aapke time ke liye."
Capture right after (3 min, while fresh): who watches + when + what-first · channels named (verbatim) · the strongest emotion word they used · what they'd switch back to · 2–3 raw quotes · the one job statement: "When ___, I want to ___, so I can ___."  Keep buckets emergent — don't pre-assign categories.
